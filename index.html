About_Protocol

I'm Austin Marshall, and welcome to Marshall Photography. I used to dislike having my picture taken, but everything changed when I started seeing the world through a camera lens. Whether it's close-up details or sweeping landscapes, I found a passion for capturing moments and subjects I care about.

I especially enjoy the editing process, transforming raw images into something new and unique. The greatest reward is seeing others enjoy what I've created. My work includes portraits, nature photography (wildlife and landscapes), and event photography for graduations, family gatherings, outings, and professional headshots. For me, taking pictures is a fulfilling way to collect memories and preserve the things that matter most.
